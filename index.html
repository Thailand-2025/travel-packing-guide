Footnotes:
1. Australian passport holders do not require a visa for stays up to 60 days.
2. Backpack or carry on bag size limit no more than 56cm (height) x 36cm (width) x 23cm (depth) including wheels and handles.
* One main item for the overhead locker (cannot exceed 7kg)
3. Small item: Fits under the seat in front of you. Common small items include:
* A purse or handbag
* A coat or blanket
* Approved duty-free goods
4. Liquid, Aerosol, and Gel Limits:
* Must be in containers of 100ml or less.
* Carried together in a transparent, resealable plastic bag.
* Bag's sealed area must add up to no more than 80cm (e.g., 20 x 20cm or 15 x 25cm).
* Only one bag per passenger (exceptions for carers).
